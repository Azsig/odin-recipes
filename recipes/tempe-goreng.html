Tempe Goreng / Fried Tempe

Description

Tempeh or tempe is a traditional Indonesian food made from fermented soybeans. It is made by a natural culturing and controlled fermentation process that binds soybeans into a cake form. A fungus, Rhizopus oligosporus or Rhizopus oryzae, is used in the fermentation process and is also known as tempeh starter.

Ingredients

  • Tempe, sliced
  • 2 Garlic, grinded
  • 50 mL water
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • Oil for frying

Direction

  1. put water and salt in grinded garlic and mix
  2. put sliced tempe in garlic water, make sure all the surface of tempe get wet by garlic water
  3. Prepare hot oil over medium heat. fry the tempe until brown
  4. Lift and drain. Fryied tempe ready to serve
